Join the beta on Garmin

For Forerunner, Fenix, Venu, Vivoactive, Epix, Instinct and other Connect IQ watches

Welcome aboard! Two quick steps and you're walking: join the testers group, then add the free Inca Trail to your Garmin from the Connect IQ store. The whole thing takes about five minutes.

Step 1 · Join the testers group

Watch Walks is in invite-only beta, so we add testers through a Google Group. Joining it unlocks the free companion phone app on Google Play during the beta.

  • Use the same Google account you use on your phone's Play Store.
  • Tap join, then accept — that's it, no forms.
  • Heads up: it can take a few minutes (occasionally an hour or two) before your access goes live.

Join the testers group

Step 2 · Add the Inca Trail to your Garmin

The Inca Trail watch app is free and live now on the Connect IQ store — you don't have to wait for the group for this part.

  • Make sure you have the free Garmin Connect app with your watch paired.
  • Open the listing below, tap Download, and pick your watch. It installs on the next sync.
  • On the watch, open Watch Walks once — it picks up today's steps and starts you down the trail.

Get the Inca Trail on Connect IQ

Want every detail? See the full Garmin setup guide.

Step 3 · (Optional) Get the companion phone app

Once your group access is live, install the free Watch Walks companion for Android. It turns your steps into a living map, unlocks a short story at every landmark, and gives you a badge for each trail you finish — reading your progress straight from your watch over Bluetooth.

Get the companion on Google Play

Trouble getting in?

  • "You're not a tester yet" or the app won't install. Give it a little longer after joining the group, and make sure you're signed in with the same Google account you used to join.
  • The trail didn't appear on my watch. Open Garmin Connect and sync again; it installs on the next sync.
  • Still stuck? Email watchwalksupport@gmail.com — we'll get you walking.
