How it works

Set it once. Then just live your life.

Every step you take in the real world carries you further along your chosen trail, automatically.

2

Walk as normal

3

Reach each milestone to unlock a short story of the place, all the way to the finish line.

Companion app

Your whole journey, in your pocket.

A free phone app, Android first and iPhone to follow, that turns your steps into a living map. Watch your position move along the real route, collect a story for every landmark you pass, and earn a badge for each trail you finish.

  • Live journey map that follows your position along the real route as your daily steps add up
  • Story postcards you unlock at every landmark and keep in your collection
  • A badge for every trail, one to earn at the finish of each route
  • Sync with your watch, updating through the day or the moment you tap Sync after a walk
  • Private by design, reading from your watch over Bluetooth and keeping everything on your phone
  • Free companion app, with no account and no server
