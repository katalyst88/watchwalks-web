How it works

Set it once. Then just live your life — every step counts.

1

Install on your Garmin watch

Add a trail from the Connect IQ store. It runs quietly in the background — no GPS, no battery drain.

2

Walk as normal

Your daily steps and distance carry you along the real route. Pull down to check your progress any time.

3

Unlock the landmarks

Reach each milestone to unlock a short story of the place — all the way to the finish line.
