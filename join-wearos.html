Join the beta on Wear OS & Samsung

For Samsung Galaxy Watch, Google Pixel Watch, Mobvoi TicWatch, OnePlus Watch and other Wear OS watches

Welcome aboard! Two quick steps and you're walking: join the testers group, then install Watch Walks from Google Play onto your watch. The Inca Trail is free.

Step 1 · Join the testers group

Watch Walks is in invite-only beta, so Google Play only lets you install it once you're in our testers group. Joining is what unlocks the download.

  • Use the same Google account that's signed in on your watch and phone Play Store.
  • Tap join, then accept — no forms, nothing to fill in.
  • Heads up: it can take a few minutes (occasionally an hour or two) before Google Play shows you the app.

Join the testers group

Step 2 · Install Watch Walks on your watch

Once you're in the group, become a tester and install the app:

  • Open the tester opt-in link below and tap Become a tester, then Download it on Google Play.
  • On your watch, open the Play Store, find Watch Walks and install it (start with the free Inca Trail).
  • Open Watch Walks on the watch once and just start walking — your everyday steps carry you down the trail, all on your wrist, no phone pairing needed.

Become a tester & install

Want every detail? See the full Wear OS & Samsung setup guide.

Step 3 · (Optional) Get the companion phone app

The free Watch Walks companion for Android turns your steps into a living map, unlocks a short story at every landmark, and gives you a badge for each trail you finish. It's the same beta access, so once you're in the group you can install it too.

Get the companion on Google Play

Trouble getting in?

  • Google Play says the app isn't available or "not a tester". Give it a little longer after joining the group, and make sure your watch/phone Play Store is signed in with the same Google account you used to join.
  • I can't find it on my watch. Make sure it installed (the watch needs a Wi-Fi or Bluetooth connection), then open the Play Store on the watch and search Watch Walks.
  • Still stuck? Email watchwalksupport@gmail.com — we'll get you walking.
